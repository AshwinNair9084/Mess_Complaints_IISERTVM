About Us

Our aim is to help individuals resolve their complaints effectively by conveying the relevant information to the appropriate authority. We understand that dealing with complaints can be a stressful and frustrating experience, which is why we are here to offer our assistance. Whether it's a complaint about mess, academics, hostel facilities, medical services, or any other issue, we are committed to ensuring that your concerns are addressed in a timely and professional manner.


Our team has a wealth of experience in handling a wide range of complaints, and we have developed effective strategies for dealing with them. We know that every complaint is unique, and we take the time to understand the specific circumstances of your situation before we begin to work on a resolution.


One of the key aspects of our approach is our commitment to conveying your complaint to the correct authority. We have a comprehensive understanding of the different channels and processes that exist for dealing with different types of complaints, and we will work closely with you to ensure that your complaint is routed to the appropriate party.


Once we have conveyed your complaint to the relevant authority, we will work closely with them to ensure that it is dealt with in a fair and timely manner. We understand the importance of keeping you informed throughout the process, and we will provide you with regular updates on the status of your complaint.


At the end of the day, our goal is to provide you with a platform where you can express your grievances and receive the necessary support to resolve them. We are committed to delivering the highest levels of service and ensuring that you are satisfied with the outcome of your complaint. So if you have a complaint about anything at all, don't hesitate to reach out to us - we are here to help!
